What is Ecoknow Gallery

Welcome to our online art gallery, where you may see provocative and thought-provoking works that address the unsustainable nature of Malaysia. Digital artworks by skilled photographersÂ who have utilised their abilities to bring attention to the problem of sustainability in Malaysia may be seen on our website.

As you browse the site, you'll come across a variety of digital artworks that show how Malaysia's environment, society, and economy have suffered due to a lack of sustainability. The artists have successfully delivered their message via a wide range of genres, including graphics, animations, films, and interactive installations.

We hope that our website inspires you to think critically about the issue of unsustainability in Malaysia and motivates you to take action to create a more sustainable future. Thank you for visiting our gallery!
